Argument validation, complex data

Validation follows a pattern similar to CGI::Ex::Validate
http://search.cpan.org/~rhandom/CGI-Ex-1.14/lib/CGI/Ex/Validate.pm

Using arrays:

./bin/demo_api_client hello value:0 5 value:1 3sir

{"value":["5","3sir"]}

Using a hash:

./bin/demo_api_client hello hash.key 5 hash.another_key 3sir

{"hash":{"key":"5","another_key":"3sir"}}
